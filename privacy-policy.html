Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

PureSpark Cleaning (“we”, “our”, “the Company”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services in Belgium.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal identification data (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Service-related information (requested service type, location, schedule)
  • Technical data (IP address, browser type, device information)
  • Communication history

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage cleaning services
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve our website and user experience
  • Send service updates or confirmations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with trusted partners or service providers only when necessary for business operations.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve website performance and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Data Protection Rights (EU/GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. You may contact us to exercise any of these rights.
